Review Buddy is an online platform where gamers can see what other people think of the games they like. You can see their top 10 lists as well as connect and communicate with others.

Review Buddy brings you the option to connect with peoplethat have the same interest with you, specifically, people that share the same love for games as you do.

Let's face it, not everyone likes the same games, and even if a game is rated 10/10, some people still don't like it. That's where we step in. Upon creating your Top 10 list, we will match you with other users that have a similar taste as you do.

Other than the features stated above, you can also read other's reviews on different games, as well as write your own! So, what are you waiting for, create an account and join others in ranting about games you hate!
